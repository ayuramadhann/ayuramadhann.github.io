Angkut Apps, Transportation Mobile Design

Fast, Easy and Safe Cross Regional Transportation Ordering Application

- Introduction

Angkut apps was initiated from an observation by a family waiting on the side of the road and a call from a friend who asked for the local driver's phone number. Which in the end became for a group of friends experience and thirst for new. From the results of the competition, a group of friends initiated this idea to include local campus startups which then resulted in the 2nd place award. Not satisfied just to be included, a group of friends made a commitment that this idea could be used as a company that can be used as well as its users, namely transportation regional car to cross the area. Currently we are very optimistic and until our idea is needed by many people and will continue to develop and create for the convenience of users.

After a few updates now the transport app has changed its name, logo and color to carryfy.
