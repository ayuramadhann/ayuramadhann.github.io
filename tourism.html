Lego Ugi, Tourism Mobile Design

- Introduction

South Sulawesi is one of the provinces in the middle of Indonesia. With the diversity of ethnic groups and languages ​​that live in harmony, it is a unique thing at Center Point Of Indonesia. Huge tourism potential, with different cultural backgrounds, topographic diversity and natural beauty, as well as support for adequate facilities make this area one of the tourist destinations. This cultural diversity is a big concern to be widely introduced, sustainable and preserved as a protection effort. For the realization of efforts to maintain and introduce sustainable cultural diversity. So an android-based tourism application design for South Sulawesi Province was made as the first guide when visiting South Sulawesi in order to explore the diversity that exists in South Sulawesi province under the name "Lego Ugi".

“Lego Ugi” comes from two syllables: Lego-lego, namely the veranda of the house and Ugi, which is Bugis. Lego – lego is the veranda of the Bugis people's house that teaches about togetherness. With this philosophy, we hope that through the design of this application, it can become a forum for all South Sulawesi people to make this application a home for them to be able to save culture so that it remains sustainable.

2. F - Design Pattern

The F-Pattern is the way our eyes move when we read online content. Within seconds, our eyes move with incredible speed across website copy and other visual elements, scanning the pages in this order:

  1. First, at the top of the page to read the headlines
  2. Then, on the left side of the page to see the numbers or bullet points
  3. Finally, across the page again to read bold text or subtitles (Stephani Mialki, 2020)
