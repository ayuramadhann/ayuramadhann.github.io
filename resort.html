- Introduction

They are located in the heart of Kollo Soha, Tomia Island. Their team is dedicated to building respect for the environment, culture and awareness within the community to provide a positive experience for visitors and their hosts. Their resort is located 50 meters from the pristine sands of Tomian beach.
